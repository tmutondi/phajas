Pfariso Mphadzha

Founder & Travel Director

Pfariso founded Phajas Travel and Tour in 2015 with a vision to create unique travel experiences for South Africans. With over 10 years in the travel industry, he has extensive knowledge of both local and international destinations.

His passion for exploring new cultures and creating meaningful connections drives the company's commitment to exceptional service. Pfariso personally oversees the planning of each trip to ensure it meets Phajas' high standards.

When not traveling, Pfariso enjoys hiking in the Drakensberg and exploring South Africa's diverse culinary scene.

Mutondi Tshivhase

Operations Manager

Mutondi joined Phajas Travel in 2017 and brings exceptional organizational skills to her role as Operations Manager. She handles all logistics, from transportation and accommodation to activity scheduling, ensuring every trip runs smoothly.

With a background in tourism management and customer service, Mutondi is dedicated to creating seamless travel experiences. Her attention to detail and problem-solving abilities have earned her praise from both colleagues and clients.

In her free time, Mutondi is an avid photographer and enjoys capturing the beauty of South Africa's landscapes.
